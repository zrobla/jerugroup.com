Power, network & UPS

Secure the operational continuity of a site before it even goes live.

JERU integrates useful power supply, structured networking and backup logic into offices, retail spaces, reception areas and professional environments. We connect load qualification, distribution, connectivity, protection and service continuity so the site works cleanly from day one.

Managed scope

From loads to be covered to operational restart.

We intervene on need qualification, useful electrical distribution, data network organization, integration of critical points and UPS logic to protect the uses that must not go down.

  • Identification of equipment, user stations and zones that must be powered or secured.
  • Organization of sockets, circuits, network points, light cabinets and useful distribution elements.
  • Dimensioning of protections and backup logic according to the expected continuity level.
  • Commissioning, functional tests and a cleaner base for site operations.
What we secure

A useful infrastructure is measured by operational reliability, not by the quantity of equipment installed.

We secure the reading of loads, the coherence between power and network, service continuity and coordination with fit-out works to avoid critical omissions, unnecessary oversizing and late technical fixes.
