Duplex villa in Akouedo, Abidjan

In Akouedo, JERU managed the construction of a duplex villa designed to offer generous volumes, optimal user comfort and a level of finishes consistent with the expectations of a demanding residential clientele.

Type Duplex villa
Location Akouedo, Abidjan
Nature New-build construction
Standing High-end finishes
Context & challenges

A villa where quality of life and perceived quality had to converge from delivery.

The project owner wanted a duplex villa that went beyond simply following a plan: every space had to be designed for daily comfort, natural circulation between levels and overall coherence between volumes, light and finishes.

Akouedo, a residential area rising in value, imposes a quality standard that will be directly compared by the neighbourhood. The villa therefore had to assert a high-end positioning visible both on the facade and in the interior.

  • Duplex with optimized day and night space distribution across two levels.
  • High-standard finishes: floors, joinery, sanitary fittings and lighting treated in detail.
  • Integrated exterior amenities for a coherent perception from the plot entrance.

JERU Intervention

Our scope of intervention on this villa.

Management & coordination

Management of all contractors, phase planning and quality monitoring at each stage to ensure a coherent result.

Structural works

Foundations, load-bearing wall construction on two levels, concrete slabs and full weatherproofing of the duplex villa.

Secondary works & networks

Code-compliant electrical installations, sanitary plumbing, aluminum joinery, railings and interior staircase.

High-end finishes

Large-format tiling, premium sanitary tiles, quality paints, false ceilings and recessed lighting for a polished result.

Plot development

Fencing, gate, access path, green space treatment and exterior lighting for a controlled overall perception.

Handover & control

Verification of each item before delivery, defect resolution and handover to the owner of an asset ready for immediate use.
