Triplex villa rehabilitation and renovation in Ouagadougou

In Ouagadougou, JERU led the complete recovery of a triplex villa to relaunch its heritage value, user comfort and overall perception. A project that demonstrated our ability to intervene outside Cote d'Ivoire with the same level of standards.

Type Triplex villa
Location Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso
Nature Major rehabilitation & renovation
Challenge Heritage value relaunch
Context & challenges

An existing asset to be fully recovered to relaunch its operation.

The owner faced a property whose overall condition no longer met the standards expected for quality residential use. The triplex villa required major intervention: structural diagnostic, network overhaul, complete modernization of finishes and reconfiguration of certain spaces.

The challenge went beyond simple cosmetic renovation. The goal was to restore the asset to a quality level that justifies its heritage value, while precisely deciding which items to keep, which to rebuild and which to modernize.

  • Intervention outside Cote d'Ivoire requiring adapted logistics and management.
  • Full existing-condition diagnostic before any recovery or preservation decision.
  • Upgrade objective without total reconstruction: optimizing the investment.
Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso

Demonstrating our execution capacity beyond the Ivorian market.

Working in Ouagadougou confirmed that JERU can deploy its management methods and quality control in a sub-regional context, without loss of rigor.

JERU Intervention

Our scope of intervention on this rehabilitation.

Diagnostic & condition survey

Full assessment of the existing condition: structural state, networks, joinery, waterproofing and finishes. Priority ranking of items by urgency and impact.

Recovery decisions

Precise definition of what is kept, rebuilt, modernized and reconfigured. Each decision was validated with the owner.

Structural recovery

Strengthening of degraded load-bearing elements, waterproofing restoration, crack treatment and consolidation of sensitive points identified during diagnostic.

Network renovation

Bringing electrical and plumbing installations up to standard, replacing aging pipes and modernizing water points.

Finishes & upgrade

New floor coverings, interior and exterior painting, modern joinery, tiles and space treatment to reach the intended standing.

Remote management

Logistics organization adapted to the Burkinabe context, structured site monitoring and coordination of local teams with JERU standards.

Results

What this rehabilitation demonstrated.

Asset relaunched

The triplex villa regained a level of quality and comfort that fully justifies its heritage value.

Sub-regional capacity

JERU proved its ability to manage a project outside Cote d'Ivoire with the same level of rigor and results.

Optimized investment

Priority ranking of items allowed the budget to focus on the highest-impact interventions.

Perception transformed

The property's image was radically improved, enabling operation under conditions consistent with the expected standing.
