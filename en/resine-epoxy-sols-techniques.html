Epoxy resin & technical flooring

Deploy durable, readable technical floors that match the real use of the space.

JERU deploys resin systems and technical flooring for environments that require mechanical resistance, simple maintenance, safety of use and visual consistency. We support parking areas, ramps, technical rooms, retail spaces, showrooms and high-traffic environments with a method built on diagnosis, specification, application and controlled return to service.

Managed scope

From existing substrate to return to circulation.

We intervene on substrate analysis, system selection, surface preparation, application, optional marking and return to use so the delivered floor is more reliable, cleaner and better aligned with its environment.

  • Reading of the substrate, humidity, flatness and mechanical constraints.
  • System specification according to traffic, maintenance, safety and expected finish.
  • Application and finishing adapted to the constraints of each environment.
  • Site planning and return to service without improvised restarts.
What we secure

A well-treated technical floor protects both performance and the image of the space.

We secure compatibility between substrate, use, traffic, maintenance, safety and final finish to avoid delamination, poorly prepared rework, unnecessary downtime and premature floor degradation.

Intervention plan

Three service entry points to analyze, specify and deploy the right flooring system.

Read the substrate correctly before promising flooring performance.

We start from the reality of the existing floor, because the performance of a resin system depends first on the quality of the substrate, its humidity, its stability, its actual use and the mechanical constraints it must absorb.

Execution method
